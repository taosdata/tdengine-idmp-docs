## Highlights

1. Events support AI-based root cause analysis to quickly identify the deepest, most fundamental, and truly controllable underlying cause of quality anomalies.
1. OPC-UA and CSV imported data support data quality bits, with data reference attributes and visualization panels capable of incorporating quality bit information.
1. Panel enhancements: more visualization chart templates, richer chart configuration options, gradient color controls added to SCADA panels, and AI-recommended panels supporting more chart types.
1. Analytics capability improvements: more advanced analytics entry points, scatter plots support clustering and predictive analysis, trend charts have improved prediction capabilities, event trend charts support envelope lines, and multi-event time-normalized comparison analysis is now supported.
1. The free trial period for the AI service has been extended from 3 days to 15 days.
1. Other minor improvements and bug fixes.
